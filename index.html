    The Taj Mahal

    Overview

    The taj Mahal is a white marble mausolem in Agra,India.It was built by Mugal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his Wife Mumtaz Mahal.

    Architecture

    The Taj Mahal is renowned for its intricate Mugal architecture,combining Persian,Indian,and Islamic styles.The main dome and four minarets are features.

    Construction

    Construction began un 1632 and took over 20 years to complete.Skilled articians and Laborers worked on the detailed marble inlays and carvings.

    Legancy

    The Taj Mahal is a UNIESO world heritage site and a symbol of love and architectural brilliance.
